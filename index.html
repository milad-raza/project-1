INTRODUCTION


a.Company History

In 1988 MR. Mohammad Zahoor Quraishi founded Al-Quraish concrete laying a strong foundation for a diversified pre-cast construction material manufacturing and general construction firm distinguished by integrity, quality, team-work and a state of the art building experience. Since it is founding, Al-Quraish concrete has experienced sustained and steady growth, with today's annual building volume reaching over 10 million and 50 employees in various projects.
The company is also registered with

  • Building research station.
    (Building department government of Pakistan)
  • Chamber of commerce & industry.
  • National productivity organization Islamabad.
  • Asian productivity organization.

b.What the company does

Al-Quraish concrete manufacture's pre-cast roofs, tough pavers, Kerb stones and construction concrete Blocks. Its target market is every project which undergoes construction or building maintenance.

Introduction




Al Quraish Concrete (Pvt) Ltd. is proud to be a pioneer in producing a widely popular system of Low-Cost Precast & Prestressed Concrete construction of roofs and of inter-floors etc. which has proved its economy, convenience, speedy building construction and good quality of work.The system has two components i.e. R.C.C. Precast Slabs and Precast Prestressed Girders.
